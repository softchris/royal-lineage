The Lineage Tree

In 1496 a child was born who would become Gustaf Vasa — liberator, king, and founder of modern Sweden. From his throne an unbroken bloodline descends through seventeen generations of royals, nobles, officers, merchants, and modern citizens, arriving at the present day. This is that bloodline, mapped from crown to kin.
